MY JOURNEY

As a dedicated and ambitious third-year Computer Science Engineering (B.Tech) student from India, I have navigated a journey filled with growth, learning, and challenges. Starting my first year with high expectations and enthusiasm, I enjoyed the initial phase with a focus on fun rather than academics. However, the second year brought the reality of academic pressures and the demands of the real world, which I managed to navigate successfully, building a solid foundation in fundamental computer science concepts. Now, in my third year, I am committed to working hard, learning continuously, and tackling the challenges that come my way. Although I am not exactly where I had initially planned to be, I have grown significantly in my ability to think critically, manage time effectively, and persevere. My technical skills include proficiency in programming languages such as Python, C, C++, Java, HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript, as well as experience with databases like SQL and MongoDB. I am eager to leverage my skills and knowledge in real-world applications and contribute meaningfully to the field of technology.
